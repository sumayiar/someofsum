Books I'm reading

  • "Happy Place" by Emily Henry
  • "The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People" by Stephen Covey

    • Happy Place

    A fiction book I'm reading by one of my favorite authors. I loved "Beach Read" so I went bananas when I saw another Emily Henry book at the library. I'm only four chapters in, as of 3/18/24, but so far it's nice. Henry's skill of adding vivid details is impeccable! It makes me want to buy a vacation house in Maine and eat loads of lobster!

    The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People" by Stephen Covey

    This is a non-fiction book that I'm reading for self-improvement. This Spring semester has been a little rough and I've been experiencing some burnout. So, this has been my handy handbook towards focusing better in my studies.

    The Alchemist

    On Eid-ul-fitr, I went to my neighbor's house— they've renovated their living room! It was beautifully furnished, with a teal couch encompassing the top left corner of their living room. But this didn't captivate me— the bookshelf next to it did! As I perused through their collection of books, I had noticed they had two copies of the Alchemist— one fairly used and one worn out. Now, seeing a classic in a Bengali household was rare and I had to see what was so special about this book. So, a couple of days later I picked this book up and voila, my current read! As of 04/14/2024, I am reading this wonderfully written book by Mr. Coelho.
